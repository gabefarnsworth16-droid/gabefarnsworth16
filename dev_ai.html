INSTRUCTIONS

RULES & MECHANICS

Manage your gold to build towers, aid your players, and outscore your opponent in a 90-minute (4 minutes of real time) soccer match. The CPU controls the opposing team. Ties go to Sudden Death.

THE PEOPLE

Elites (Solid Colors): Your core 4 players (1 Goalie, 1 Anchor, 2 Roamers). They are immune to all damage and cannot be killed, but can be pushed around from time to time.
Reinforcements (Light Colors): Extra players spawned from your towers. They fight enemies, but can be killed by opposing reinforcements, towers, and spectators.
Spectators (Dark Colors): Soccer fans. Gotta love 'em. Since they are indeed crazy, they invade the pitch every 3 goals the opposing team scores...oh, and they can kill enemy reinforcements and destroy towers. You cannot stop them. You can only hope to contain them.

PURCHASE OPTIONS

Push Tower ($50): Fires pulses that knock back enemies and deal light damage to reinforcements.
Freeze Tower ($75): Fires pulses that temporarily freeze enemies and deal heavy damage to reinforcements.
Artillery ($100): Fires globally at enemy towers to destroy them. Cannot target players.
Defender Spawner ($150): Spawns defensive reinforcements that guard your half of the field. (Limit 1).
Attacker Spawner ($200): Spawns aggressive reinforcements that hunt enemies and try to score. If an Attacker scores, they become a permanent Elite! (Limit 1).

SPECIAL FUNCTIONS

The Cavalry ($500): Sends 30 of your spectators to swarm the pitch, instantly destroying enemy towers on contact. Ends after your team scores 3 goals.
Last Stand ($150): Available only if the enemy scores 1 goal during their Cavalry run. Sends 30 of your spectators to wrestle the enemy's Cavalry.
Armageddon ($750): Available if you trail by 7+ goals in the 2nd half. Ends the soccer match and starts a 30v30 spectator battle to the death. You have zero control over the battle, you can only watch helplessly. So if you're up by a lot, make sure the opposition doesn't save enough money...oh, and cost drops to $200 if trailing by 10+ in the final 30 seconds (of real time).
